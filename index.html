How to Play

3 in a row, or Tic-Tac-Toe, is a simple game. The players take turns placing their markers on the game board. The goal is to get 3 of your markers in a row, while using your markers to block your opponent's.

Click the start button to bring up the game board. Then select your token and place it in a square by clicking that square
